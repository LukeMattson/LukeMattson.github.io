Who We Are

  • Name: The Cardiff Cup
  • Founded: 2025
  • Location: Cardiff city centre
  • Mission: To reimagine the traditional British café with bold, creative, and locally-inspired twists, while remaining warm, welcoming, and community-focused.

What We Serve

  • Traditional Full English & Welsh Breakfast
  • House-baked cakes (e.g. Sticky Toffee Cupcake & Welsh Cake Stack)
  • Artisan teas & barista-crafted coffee
  • Creative drinks like Freakshakes & Spiced Apple Lattes
  • Vegan, veggie & gluten-free options available

Who We Serve

  • For everyone: early morning regulars, weekend brunch-goers, families, students, freelancers, and tourists
  • Kids’ Corner with a special menu, babyccinos, and family-friendly seating

What Makes Us Different

  • No direct competitors – the only café of its kind in the area
  • Seasonal specials inspired by Welsh culture & global trends
  • Cosy yet modern interior perfect for socialising or relaxing
  • Eco-conscious: biodegradable packaging, local produce, reusable cup discounts

Our Location

Located in the heart of Cardiff, just a short walk from Queen Street and Cardiff Castle. Look for the golden kettle sign above the door!

Community & Values

  • Independent & proudly local
  • Host to local art, live music & seasonal events
  • Built on inclusive hospitality and comfort food with soul
  • Dedicated to supporting the Cardiff community
